About JTK Coffee Shop

JTK coffee shop was founded by 3 males in the western parts of South Africa, Cape Town. The founders, Jayden, Kassami, and Taulha, aimed to make mornings special for fellow Capetonians with well-crafted coffee. Our mission is to create exceptional coffee experiences while fostering a sense of community.
